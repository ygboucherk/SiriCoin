What is SiriCoin?

SiriCoin is a ERC-20 based coin, created using Polygon-Network.

Features of SiriCoin

1:- As of now SiriCoin can be mined with almost every device that can connect to internet with browser.

2:- You could also mine the low-powered devices like, Mobiles, Old Pc, RaspberryPi, Smart-TV's, etc.

3:- You need not have any Linux Knowledge to Mine SiriCoin, You start mining with our official Web-Miner.

4:- Our Future updates will include hardware mining(which will allow you to mine with your CPU, etc..using officially made python miner).

Why Should you Mine SiriCoin & Not other Crypto's?

1:- SiriCoin is Beginner-Friendly.

2:- Easy to Mine.

Block Time:- 150 Secs.

Ticker:- SC.

Consensus:- PoW (Keccak256).
