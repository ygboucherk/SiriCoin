What is SiriCoin?

SiriCoin is a ERC-20 based coin, created using Polygon-Network.

Features of SiriCoin

1:- SiriCoin can be mined with almost everydevice that can connect to internet with browser.

2:- You could also mine the low-powered devices like, Raspberry-Pi's, Mobiles, Old Pc, etc.

3:- Also SiriCoin doesn't any hardware configurations as it can be mined with Official Web-Miner(which uses JavaScript to mine).

4:- Our Future updates will include hardware mining(which will allow you to mine with your CPU, etc..will be planned).
